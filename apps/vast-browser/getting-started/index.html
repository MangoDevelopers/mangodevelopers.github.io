Welcome! Here's everything you need to browse the web with your remote.

🎯Move the cursor

  • Use the D-pad to move the on-screen cursor. Hold a direction — the cursor starts precise and speeds up the longer you hold.
  • Press OK to click whatever is under the cursor.
  • Push the cursor past the edge of the screen and the page scrolls with it.
💡 Adjust the top speed in Settings → Cursor & Scrolling.

📜Scroll pages

  • Hold OK for about a second to enter scroll mode — the cursor becomes a disk and the D-pad now scrolls the page smoothly.
  • Hold OK again (or press Back) to return to the normal cursor.

🔍Address bar & search

  • Press ▲ Up three times quickly to open the address bar. (The Menu button works too.)
  • Type a web address, or just type words to search the web.
  • Press ▼ Down three times to hide the bar again.
  • The 🏠 button takes you home — long-press it on any page to make that page your new home page.

🛡️Ads & trackers are blocked for you

  • VastBrowser blocks ads, analytics and social trackers at the network level — before they even load. It's on by default.
  • See the running totals (and switch it off if you want) in Settings → Ad & Tracker Blocking.
Try the live ad-block test →

🎬Media keys

  • Play/Pause controls any video on the page.
  • ⏩ / ⏪ jump 10 seconds forward or back.

🔄Stay up to date

  • VastBrowser checks for updates automatically and shows a dot on the ⚙️ settings button when a new version is ready.
  • Install updates in Settings → About & Updates, no app store needed.

Exit the browser any time by pressing Back three times.
